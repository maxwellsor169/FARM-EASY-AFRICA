GENERAL FARMING TIPS

  • Always have a general plan for the whole farming year
  • Make sure all the resources you will need are readily available
  • Its always good to turn the soil with all leftover debris and also apply some animal manure during land preparation
  • Always source out for a mentor if you are new to farming, it helps reduce losses and mistakes.
  • Note that mulching in vegetable production always helps in soil moisture conservation.
  • You can develop a mobile irrigation system for your farm if you are not close to a water source, this helps farms to cultivate through out the year
  • Always make sure to keep records of your farm income and expenditure account.
  • Make room for 2 to 3 months fallow period for the farm land to replenish its lost nutrient, as it helps good crop yield.
